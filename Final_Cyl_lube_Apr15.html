Reciprocating Compressor Cylinders lube Oil Consumption

The calculation helps to estimate Cylindres lube oil consumption in reciprocating compressors.
Default is based on fixed lube rate of 0.008 L/day/mm of bore diameter with multiplier.
Check the Compressor manufacturer lube rate and change the default as required.

Flange Pressure - Temperature Rating, Carbon Steel
As temperature increases, the pressure rating of the flange decreases.

For example, a Class 150 flange is rated to approximately 270 PSIG at ambient conditions, 180 PSIG at approximately 400°F, 150 PSIG at approximately 600°F, and 75 PSIG at approximately 800°F.





Basis of calculation

Actual volume, initial pressure and initial temperature can be obtained from settle out pressure calculation.
It is always a good practice to install orifice plates downstream of blowdown valves, that gives flexibility to reduce the blowdown time if a blowdown valve has a larger trim than required.

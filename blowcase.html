Blowcase Sizing


Typical Blowcase has 4 connections with control valves as follows
1- Liquid Fill nozzle: to dump liquid upstream of the compressor
2- Liquid dump nozzle: dump liquids downstream of the compressor
3- Balance line: connected with suction scrubber or inlet separator
4- Power line: connected with compressor discharge upstream of AC
The driving force required to push the liquids out of the blowcase is obtained from delta pressure cause by the AfterCooler.
increse "the flow rate by %age" until match the AC pressure drop
Orifice plates may be required on the compressor dischargein order to increse liquid dump rates.
